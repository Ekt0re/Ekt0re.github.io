Privacy Policy

HITS Games respects the privacy of its users. The purpose of this policy is to explain how we treat users' personal information.

Data Collection

HITS Games does not collect or retain any kind of personal information from users, either through its Web sites or through its video games. Users can freely browse our site without having to provide any personal information.

OAuth Consent Screen

In The Fallen Agent and Ghost Run, the only login request with OAuth is used exclusively to connect the game to Google Play Games services. This connection enables features such as trophies and global leaderboards, provided directly by Google Play Games.

HITS Games does not in any way collect, store, or access personal information related to the user's Google account. The information used for trophies and leaderboards is handled completely by Google Play Games, in accordance with their privacy policy.

No Data Collection

At no time does HITS Games collect or store personal information about users, either on its Web sites or within its video games. We do not collect information about visitors to our site, use tracking cookies, or integrate data analysis services.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to make changes to this privacy policy. Any changes will be promptly posted on this page.
