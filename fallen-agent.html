About the Game

"The Fallen Agent" is a 2D action shooter game set in the year 2235. You play as Mark, a special forces agent sent to planet H-001 to destroy the cybernetic network of terrorists and prevent the spread of a powerful computer virus!

Navigate through intense action sequences, upgrade your weapons, and use strategic thinking to overcome enemy defenses in this retro-styled sci-fi adventure.
